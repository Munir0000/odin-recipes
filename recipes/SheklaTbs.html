Shekla TBS

Description

Shekla Tibs is a traditional Ethiopian dish made from succulent pieces of meat (often beef or lamb) sautéed with onions, tomatoes, and a blend of spices. It is typically served in a clay dish that keeps the food warm, and it’s often enjoyed with injera, a spongy flatbread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef or lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon berbere spice mix
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Injera, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced onion and sauté until it begins to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the beef or lamb pieces to the skillet and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, berbere spice mix, ground ginger, salt, and pepper.
  6. Reduce the heat to medium and let the mixture simmer for about 15-20 minutes, until the meat is cooked through and the flavors are well combined.
  7. Serve hot, ideally in a clay dish, accompanied by injera.
